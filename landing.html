01Social contagion over rational expectation+

Investors are not independent. Every node in our network feels its neighbors. The Barabási-Albert topology means hub investors drag the herd. This is what actually happens.

02Signal, not noise+

The model abstains when |h| < 0.15. Weak signal is not a prediction. 85.2% live accuracy comes from calling only when the sentiment field is clear.

03Event-type aware+

Earnings, Fed, CPI, drug trials, mergers — each has its own keyword filter, polarity, and fusion weights. A biotech PDUFA is not an earnings print.

04Built for the edge case+

Small-cap tickers with no coverage. Macro events where positive sentiment means hawkish = bearish for rate cuts. The model handles the inversion.
